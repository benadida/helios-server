To cast a vote, you will be led through the following steps. If you have not yet logged in, you will be asked to do so at the very end of the process.

  1. Select your preferred options.
    You can easily navigate forwards and backwards.

  2. Review & confirm your choices.
    Your choices are encrypted safely inside your browser, and you get a smart ballot tracker.

  3. Submit your encrypted choices.
    You will be asked to log in to submit your encrypted choices.
